Driving operational performance in manufacturing
  • March 31, 2017 | Brochure

In today’s digitized business environment, industries across the globe see an opportunity to improve the performance of their production systems. New technologies bring significant changes in the way we orchestrate factory of the future. Whatever the maturity of an organization, it is never too late to leverage digital technologies and progressively embrace Industry 4.0 transformation. According to Patrice Le Franc, Global Head of Manufacturing Operations Management (MOM), “Smart MOM solutions and architectures from Capgemini connect industrial objects and people with business processes.”

Based on such observations, we have gathered the business requirements to drive Factory 4.0 and created its vision of Smart MOM by demonstrating how the usual functions of MES or MOM can be amplified and supplemented by digital technologies. Download to read more.
