Krishnamurthy Voora

Executive Vice President & Global Head (ADM, ERP & AUTO PRACTICES)

Mr. Krishnamurthy Voora has close to three decades of experience in providing leadership in the IT industry. His work experience spreads across industry sectors such as Financial services, life sciences, healthcare and retail and in his entire 28 years of career span, he has a proven track record in delivery management, competency & capability development, solution building, operations management & driving workforce transformation. He is also the sponsor for Tech Fiesta event for Financial Services business at Capgemini.

Antoine Imbert

Chief Operating Officer - India, Practices and Transformation, Capgemini

Antonie Imbert is the joint Chief Operating Officer of Capgemin in India. Since 2016, Antoine was leading the Insights & Data practice in France and was a member of Application Services France executive committee. In this position, he notably drove the portfolio rotation towards Artificial Intelligence and the new data landscape. He joined the Group in 2014 to work for Paul Hermelin, CEO, on some of the Group’s strategic initiatives. Antoine started his career in the French public sector in 2007, as a member of the French Court of auditors, where he was in charge of controlling the Energy and Defense departments.

Antoine studied at the University of California, Berkeley, and holds a Master from IEP Paris. He is also a graduate in France from Ecole Nationale d’Administration (ENA).

Patrick Nicolet

Group Chief Technology Officer - Member of the Group Executive Board

In January 2018, Patrick Nicolet has been appointed Chief Technology Officer (CTO), in charge of the technology and innovation agenda for the Group. He also oversees the Group internal Information Systems and Cybersecurity.

Most recently, from January 2016, Patrick created the Group’s Competitiveness Program where he regrouped Delivery, Industrialization, Procurement and Internal IT into a single platform to generate significant productivity savings across the organization. He also supervised Capgemini’s India operations since the acquisition of iGate ensuring a successful integration of 30,000 iGate colleagues with the existing 60,000 Capgemini India colleagues.

Between January 2011 and December 2015, Patrick was the CEO of Infrastructure Services and Executive leader for supervising Capgemini’s operations in Latin America.

He was appointed CEO of Nordic and Central Europe Application Services in 2009. He joined Capgemini in 2000 and held significant roles delivering business development/business transformation as the Sales Director for Capgemini’s Telecom & Media business and subsequently, as the Group Sales Director.

Patrick began his career as the General Manager of the first Swiss private-public partnership company to develop an incubator, technology transfer services and a science park. He then worked as an independent turnaround manager for several European industrial corporations following which he became a partner at the Corporate Recovery practice of Ernst & Young Consulting, Switzerland.

Patrick graduated with a degree in Law from the University of Lausanne in 1984 as major of his promotion. He was honored in 1993 as Global Leader for Tomorrow by the World Economic Forum.

Nisheeth Srivastava

Chief Technology and Innovation Officer – India, Capgemini

Nisheeth Srivastava is responsible for driving Capgemini’s agenda for technology and innovation by leveraging sectoral and cross-domain expertise and the rapidly evolving technology landscape, thereby expanding and enhancing the role of India in making Capgemini the preferred innovation partner for clients. As the Chief Technology & Innovation Officer from India, Nisheeth leads the Applied Innovation Exchanges and Sector Hubs in India to create a powerful value proposition for strategic client footprint while additionally building an enabling ecosystem of partners to help power our global Innovation network.

In a career spanning 25 years, Nisheeth has been a seasoned entrepreneur as well as a corporate leader with global experience and specializes in executing new go-to-market and capability expansion initiatives, through collaborative and innovative strategies. He has founded, invested in and mentored a spectrum of start-ups and is on the advisory board of several startup incubators and Innovation Hubs in India. In his prior corporate roles, he has also led large revenue management roles in various IPP firms in Europe and India, with significant experience in Financial Services industry, bringing value-centricity to the mandate.

Nisheeth is an engineering graduate in Electronics and Communications from IIT (BHU), Varanasi. An entrepreneur at heart, he believes that embracing and learning from frequent failures is more important than celebrating the rare successes.

He is passionate about Diversity & Inclusion as well as CSR; and contributes in personal capacity towards the causes of child education and rehabilitation.
