Fabien Pierrel

IT Transformation Director at Chanel Tech

With more than 15 years of experience on both operational functions (Supply Chain & Finance) and technological ecosystems, Fabien’s expertise is recognized in leading large-scale and complex projects within international context.

Over the last decade, Fabien’s main focus was on global/strategic processes, local statutory/operation requirements and change management strategies. Travelling across the globe, he handled the design and implementation of various Core Models solutions, joining business processes and associated IT solutions. His initial connection with India started in 2012, within the Industrial sector and then continued in 2018 within the Luxury sector, by contributing to Chanel Tech strategy.

Since 2020, Fabien has been appointed as IT Transformation Director to support Chanel stakeholders and preferred IT partners in their transformation journey (scalability, expertise, reactivity and innovation) to enhance customer’s experience.

Saurabh Chandra

Managing Director with BCG India

Saurabh Chandra, a bay area expatriate, has 22+ years of professional experience in multiple CXO & executive management roles managing technology, product having P&L responsibilities with companies such as Amazon, FlipKart Group companies, Visa, JPMC, Oracle. He has been an entrepreneur, founded 2 internet companies and exited successfully. He holds 7 patents and trade secrets, is an author of 2 books by McGraw Hill and holds 5 research papers. He sits on board & advisory of 3 technology companies, had been advisor to Google Incubator and visiting faculty to few Ivy league B-Schools.

Clifton Menezes

India Head of Group Portfolio, Capgemini India

Clifton Menezes is India Head of Group Portfolio, ADMNext Group Offer Leader and also governs India Alliances and India Sustainibility CoE. As a technology evangelist with a strong focus on client centricity, Clifton loves solving customer problems with technology solutions in a sustainable way.

Within the experience of 35 years + across 14 countries, he has been associated with Capgemini for 10+ years. Prior to joining Capgemini, he was with TCS for 25 years. He is also a chairman of IFCCI – Technology committee.

He holds a master’s degree in Computer Science & Financial Management from JBIMS, Mumbai University. He is passionate about reading books, sustainability and sports.

Sarika Naik

Chief Marketing Officer & Chairperson, Diversity – India, Capgemini

Sarika is the Chief Marketing Officer and Chairperson for D&I at Capgemini Technology Services, India. She bears nearly two decades of rich and diverse industry experience, with a rare combination of global marketing, sales & corporate functions.

At Capgemini, she is responsible for enhancing the Brand amongst customers, colleagues and industry at large. She has been also playing an active role in shaping the organization’s diversity practices, and is committed towards building a more inclusive workplace at Capgemini. Sarika is also a Capgemini Expert in Corporate Functions, Front Line Sales, and Global Marketing.

Prior to joining Capgemini, Sarika was the Global Head of Marketing for Infrastructure and Cloud Computing business and Customer Experience Head for Dell Services, India. While leading the marketing function, she has pioneered initiatives to enhance organization’s brand presence through integrated marketing programs and was part of Dell India Employee Welfare board. Prior to Dell, she was with Wipro for over 15 years having worn many hats in roles spanning sales, process excellence, customer advocacy and marketing for Global Infrastructure Services and Wipro InfoTech. Sarika was also the Ombudsperson & Head of PSHC (Prevention of Sexual Harassment Committee) based on the credibility & high integrity demonstrated over the years at Wipro.

She holds a Bachelor’s degree in Electronics Engineering and a Master’s degree in Marketing Management. Sarika is also a certified Six Sigma Black Belt in DMAIC, LEAN and TQSS Methodologies.

Sanjay Amesur

Sanjay has 24 years of rich and in-depth SAP experience that demonstrates ability to lead, conceptualize, and align technology & organizational initiatives to strengthen competitiveness of the business. Elisabetta Spontoni

Group Offer Leader Digital Core with SAP S/4HANA and Global Head of SAP, Capgemini

Elisabetta is Vice President in Capgemini, with 28 years’ experience in ICT governance, projects and operations.

She has spent most of her career in SAP business both in delivering complex global projects with growing responsibilities and in presales as bid director in multiple international deals, as well as in the role of portfolio leader. She has strong program/project management skills and helped multiple clients in setting up their enterprise portfolio management on IT projects. Her key strengths are a recognised leadership, ability to work collaboratively with colleagues, clients and partners in multicultural teams, sharp ICT background, with a focus on SAP technologies, delivery excellence, innovation and executive-level communication.

Andre Cichowlas

Deputy Head OTI and Member of the Group Executive Committee, Capgemini

André has worked at Capgemini since 1985 in many roles including as a software engineer, project manager, security expert, skill center manager and CTO. Andre was Head of Delivery for the Capgemini Group from November 2014 to March 2021. His responsibility covered management of risks in all our engagements, definition of the methods, management of quality, processes for financial and operational management of engagements and the people supply chain looking at the optimisation of fulfilment and the forecasting of skills.

Prior to this, since 2013 he was Head of Global Practices for the Financial Services Global Business Unit. In this role, he focused on issues critical to the financial services industry, managing the portfolio and delivery model of the Global Business Unit.

André has kept a constant interest in the delivery of IT services in all industries and has managed IT programs and enterprise architecture engagements, in addition to IT project reviews and redesigns associated with large-scale systems implementations.

André holds a degree in engineering from the Ecole Polytechnique (1983) and in IT engineering from Ecole Supérieure d’Electricité (1985).
